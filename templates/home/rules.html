Rules & Regulations

Welcome to PlayEsports.in! Our platform is dedicated to hosting competitive tournaments for BGMI (Battlegrounds Mobile India) and Free Fire, offering exciting cash prizes and rewards. To ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for everyone, we request all participants to carefully read and abide by the following rules and regulations.

General Rules

Eligibility

  • Participants must register with valid information, including a unique in-game username and ID.
  • Age limit: Participants must be at least 16 years old to compete.
  • Cheaters, banned players, or those with known misconduct in past tournaments are not eligible.

Team Composition

  • Each team must consist of the required number of players (e.g., 4 for BGMI Squad mode or 2 for Free Fire Duo mode).
  • Substitute players are allowed only if registered before the tournament begins.

Tournament Format

  • Matches will follow the format specified during registration (e.g., TPP/FPP, Solo/Duo/Squad).
  • Points will be awarded based on kills and placement, as outlined in the event details.

Communication

  • All official communication will take place on the PlayEsports.in app/website or via official WhatsApp/Discord channels.
  • Teams must join the provided room/lobby within the specified time to avoid disqualification.

Game-Specific Rules

BGMI Rules

  • Map Selection: Matches may take place on maps like Erangel, Miramar, Sanhok, or Vikendi, as specified in the tournament.
  • Cheating/Exploits: The use of cheats, hacks, exploits, or third-party tools is strictly prohibited.
  • Fair Play:
    • No teaming with other players outside your squad.
    • Any use of emulators is prohibited. Only mobile devices are allowed.
  • Ping Issues: Participants must ensure a stable internet connection. Matches will not be paused or restarted due to individual ping issues.

Free Fire Rules

  • Devices: Only mobile devices are allowed; emulator usage is banned.
  • Cheats/Mods: Any form of cheating, including aimbots, wallhacks, or modified APKs, will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Stream Sniping: Watching live streams to gain an unfair advantage is strictly prohibited.
  • Loadout: Players must use standard game settings, as customized mods are not allowed.

Code of Conduct

  • Sportsmanship:
    • Toxic behavior, harassment, or offensive language will not be tolerated.
    • Respect fellow players, organizers, and viewers at all times.
  • Integrity: Players are expected to compete to the best of their abilities without any intention to manipulate match outcomes.
  • Accountability: Teams are responsible for ensuring their members adhere to the rules. Any team found violating rules will face penalties, including point deductions, match forfeiture, or banishment from future tournaments.

Prize Distribution

  • Winners must provide accurate payment information (e.g., UPI/Bank details) for cash rewards.
  • Prizes will be distributed within 7 business days after verifying the winners' credentials.
  • Any disputes regarding prize distribution must be raised within 48 hours of the tournament's conclusion.

Disqualification

Disqualification

  • Players or teams may be disqualified for:
    • Using unregistered players or substitutes.
    • Failure to join the lobby on time.
    • Violating any game-specific rules or the code of conduct.

Reporting Issues

If you encounter any issues during the tournament, including cheating or rule violations, report them immediately to the organizers via the official PlayEsports.in Discord or support email at support@playesports.in.

By participating in our tournaments, you agree to abide by these rules. PlayEsports.in reserves the right to update the rules at any time to maintain fairness and transparency.
